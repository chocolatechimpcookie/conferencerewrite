About the Conference

History

When World Congress CS-IT Conference was founded in 1975, computers still were mostly terminals and the layman did not have access. Today, the conference gathers crowds of 30,000 people each year in the many countries that it is visits.

Goal

The goal of the World Congress CS-IT Conference is educating the professional programmer, IT person as well as the layman. We broadcast live on the internet and are hosted on YouTube. We cover both history and new technology. Hardware and software. Mobile and super computers. We do it all.
